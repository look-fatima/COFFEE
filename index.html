Customer's Review

Jonas Blue

The taste of coffee is stunning,
subtle and smooth flavor.

I recently tried the Dark Roast from Blue Mountain Coffee Co., and I have to say, it was a game-changer for my mornings. The rich aroma hit me as soon as I opened the bag, promising a robust and flavorful experience. The taste didn't disappoint – deep, chocolatey notes with a hint of smokiness, just the way I like it. The only reason I'm giving it four stars instead of five is that it was a tad too strong for my wife, who prefers a milder blend. Overall, a fantastic choice for anyone who enjoys a bold cup of coffee to kickstart their day.

Emily

The taste of coffee is stunning,
subtle and smooth flavor.

I decided to try the Ethiopian Yirgacheffe from Coffee Lovers Club, and it has quickly become my go-to afternoon pick-me-up. The flavor profile is incredibly unique, with bright, fruity notes and a lovely floral aroma. It’s not too acidic, making it smooth and easy to drink, even without any sweeteners or milk. I love that it’s also organic and fair trade, so I feel good about my purchase. This coffee is perfect for those who appreciate a lighter, more complex brew. Five stars, hands down!

Michael

The taste of coffee is stunning,
subtle and smooth flavor.

recently sampled the Italian Espresso blend from Café Roma, and I have mixed feelings about it. On the one hand, it has a rich, creamy texture and a strong, intense flavor that espresso lovers would appreciate. The crema was perfect, and it had a nice balance of bitterness and sweetness. However, I found it a bit too bitter for my taste, even when made as a latte. It also left a somewhat lingering aftertaste that I didn't love. It's a decent espresso, but not my favorite. I'd recommend it to those who enjoy a more traditional, robust espresso experience. Three stars from me.
